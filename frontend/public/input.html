About Us

We are a website that helps blind people recognize and learn about the colors of fruits.

By entering the fruit name you want to know (you can start typing immediately when entering the website), then pressing the Enter key or clicking the Speak button.

The website will speak the fruit's color for you.

We currently support fruits in five colors: Red, Yellow, Green, Orange, and Purple, as well as over 150 types of fruits.
